ALL ELECTRONICS

All electronics can be repaired or repurposed.




GREEN-E

Repairing electronics helps to conserve natural resources. When we buy new devices, we contribute to the demand for raw materials needed to manufacture them. By repairing and reusing our existing devices, we reduce the need for new materials and minimize the environmental impact of electronic production.

FAQ


What should we do with our old electronics?
Repair
Repairing electronics can be as simple as replacing a battery or more advance like fixing a broken motherboard.
Refurbish
Got an electronic that is worn out? Refurbishing electronics is the next step! This usually consist of replacing and repairing numerous parts until it's almost brand new.
Recycle
Sadly, not everything can be repaired. Some devices are too old or completely broken? Recycling is your best bet!
