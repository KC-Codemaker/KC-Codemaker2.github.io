Don't throw it away!

Throwing away electronics, also known as electronic waste or e-waste, can have several negative consequences for the environment and human health.

Repair and Recycle!

Repairing and recycling electronics can help conserve natural resources, reduce greenhouse gas emissions, save consumers money, create jobs, and demonstrate social responsibility. It also helps comply with legal requirements for electronic waste disposal. This is our main goal!
